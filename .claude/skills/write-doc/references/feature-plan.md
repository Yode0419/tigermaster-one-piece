# Feature Planning Document Template

For new features or optimization of existing ones. Typically produced after a `/luffy` design session.

```markdown
# [功能名稱]

## 概述

- **類型**：新功能 / 既有功能優化
- **受影響角色**：客戶 / 師傅 / 客服

---

## 問題定義

### 痛點
[What is broken or missing, from the affected role's perspective]

### 現況
[How this is handled today — including any manual workarounds]

### 成功標準
[What "fixed" looks like from the user's perspective]

---

## 設計方向

[The chosen design approach, and why this direction over the alternatives]

---

## 影響範圍

[Which existing flows, rules, or documents are affected or need to change]

---

## 待釐清事項（TBD）

- [ ] [Open questions or unresolved details]

---

## 決策記錄（如適用）

[Key design decisions made during planning, if not filed as a separate ADR.
Format: 決定了什麼 → 為什麼 → 放棄了什麼替代方案]
```
